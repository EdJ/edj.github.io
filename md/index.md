# Jolt Groovebox

- Jolt is a mobile beat machine, designed for building percussive grooves and electric melodies
- Inspired by modular synthesizers and hardware grooveboxes
- Designed and built for iOS

https://testflight.apple.com/join/9cggCm44 - website
https://testflight.apple.com/join/AgrYFn3Y - forums


## Pocket beats, massive sound

- Jolt was designed from the ground up to be the mobile beat station that you keep coming back to.
- At its core, it’s 8 voices, each with their own step sequencer, with per-section and per-sequence parameter automation, as well as per-instrument LFOs.


## Samples or synths, you choose

- Each voice can play any one of the built-in instruments on any step
- Break the limits of the 8 voices by mixing multiple instruments into a single pattern

### Classic Synth
- Wavetable synth with 4 modes: spread, 2-op fm, paraphony, or automatically in-scale chords

### Modal Synth
- 16 sub-synths, based on Mutable Instruments Plaits, from harmonic waveforms to modelled analog drums

### Sampler
- Pitched or slicing sampler, with old-skool repitching and per-step sample reversing. Record from your onboard microphone, or load in mp3/wav samples

### Drum synth
- Powerful drum synth, capable of making clicky hi-hats, crushing snares, or pounding kicks



## Effect Chain
- Any instrument can have any nunmber of effects attached, in any order

### Overdrive
- Crushing distortion, great for making powerful leads or spicing up your drums

### Saturator
- Light saturator for bringing a bit of warmth, or heavy saturation to bring some fuzz

### Chorus
- Make your pads shimmer, and your basses bubble

### Filter
- 4-stage ladder filter emulation, or resonant SVF low- high- or band-pass. Equiped with its own mod envelope

### Band/EQ
- Simple 4-band EQ to quickly build space in your mix, or use a custom band to let your sound find its space

### Erode
- Bring a grainly, lo-fi feel to any sound

### Bitcrush
- Cut the bit depth, for that old-school sound

### Sample rate reduction
- Reduce the sample rate, ??

### Phaser
- Add some movement to your pads or bass

### Gater
- Synced 16-step configurable gate, for stuttering drums or the classic "trance-gate" sound

### Stereo Pan
- Pan or increase the width of any element to build space in your mix



## Send effects
- Every instrument can be sent to one of the global effects, each of which is fully configurable

### Reverb
- Classic plate reverb, with a configurable space from short slap-backs to long tails

### Delay
- Synced delay with ping-pong and feedback

### Multi-tap
- Synced delay with 8 taps, great for adding rhythm to stabs or percussive sounds

### Sidechain
- Sidechain any element from your kick, including the other global effects, adding that "pump" or just building space for your kick


## Global Effect Macros
- Build up to 5 effect macros, bringing one-touch build-ups and breakdowns into your performance. Mix and match from classic performance effects

- Reverb
- Delay
- Phaser
- Beat repeat
- Resonant DJ-style low-pass filter
- Resonant DJ-style high-pass filter


## Modulate and automate everything
- Instrument parameters, and global effects, can be automated to bring movement to your track. Every automatable parameter can be automated by multiple modulation sources

### Per-instrument LFO
- Each instrument has a freely-assignable LFO, with shapes including sine, ramp, or random

### Pattern automation lane
- Draw or design synced automation on any pattern, linked to any instrument parameters

### Song automation lane
- Automate changes from bar-to-bar, or override in real time with a fader, linked to any instrument parameters and to the performance effects

## Explore, sequence, jam
- Build patterns with up to 64 steps, by programming steps, playing in midi notes, or with generative algorithms
- Chain patterns and attach automation to make longer songs
- Combine patterns in performance mode and jam, with live control over instrument parameters and all your effects
